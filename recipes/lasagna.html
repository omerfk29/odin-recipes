Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is possibly the best food ever invented. It contains protein, carbs, fat, all while tasting delicious.

Ingredients

  • A bunch of cheese
  • Some beef
  • tomato sauce
  • A bunch of different cheese
  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Seasoning stuff

Steps

  1. Get a large oven-ready glass pan
  2. Cook the beef and sauce it up
  3. Boil the pasta
  4. Grate that cheese
  5. Add first layer of pasta
  6. Second layer beef
  7. Third layer cheese and repeat
  8. Make sure to add sauce in between
  9. Toss that bad boy into the oven and bake
  10. Your lasagna should be done
